Upcoming Events

First General Meeting of 2020

January 15th, 7 PM

The first SEDS general meeting will be taking place on January 15th at 7 PM. We will be holding a mid year re-election for a few officer positions. Also we will go over some of the projects that we will be taking on this semester. At the end we will also have some time for an explosively fun game, so make sure your in the iMakerspace at 7 PM on January 15th

Gainesboro Crater

Spring 2020

TSEDS will be visiting the Flynn Creek Crater in Gainesboro. The crater is 3.8 km in diameter and is 360 million years old. The tour will be led by our very own Dr. Wolak. Dates and details for this tour are still being determined so stay tuned for more information.

SEDS 2020 T-Shirts

February 22nd

SEDS wil be starting the semester off right with a new line of designer T-Shirts. T-Shirts will be $15 for one or $25 for two. You can pick and choose between a few different styles here. The club will be placing order on February 22nd, so make sure to get your order in before then.
